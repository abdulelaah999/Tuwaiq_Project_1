Therapeutic departments

The center contains various sections for all cases

Mechanical therapy

The Department of Mechanical Therapy is a physical therapy department that uses body movements and relies on one of nature's most effective forces, i.e. movement. Motor therapy is included in several therapeutic exercises, both positively and negatively. Mechanical therapy is one of the most effective treatments if followed regularly.

Treatment in the pool

It is a treatment that helps weak muscles during movement, when the body floats above water. In this case, water resistance creates a certain type of balanced resistance exercise that helps the body regain vitality after feeling weak, during recovery periods or after surgery..

Treatment of crane ropes

It is an alternative treatment for fitness activities or aerobics exercises. This can be applied with the processor. It also combines a session of effective exercise with modern rehabilitation methods.
